Projects

Kompendix is an online source of knowledge about algorithms and data structures. Each lesson consists of an article and a creative assignment. Most of them include a lecture as well. Using Kompendix will teach you analytical thinking and alternative problem solving. You will not only prepare for Computer Science contests, but also grasp the thinking process needed for professional programming.

Tutoring

Receive help from widely experienced, excellent olympiad laureates and learn how to program. After applying to our program, you will join an online study group. For about two months you will be getting introduced to C++ language and basic algorithms and data structures. Afterwards, we will assign you to an individual tutor who can tailor the study path to your personal needs and wishes. Both the way of communication and the task level will depend on your own preference, abilities and objective. No matter what your goal is, we will supply you with the knowledge necessary to reach it, whether you want to win a contest, prepare for a future job or pursue your passion. Your tutor will be more than willing to help.

NeXtStopIT IT is a contest that strives to promote individual approach to widely understood programming. If you're a high school or a middle school student, show us, which area is the one you're most passionate about and win prestigious awards. Prizes include an internship in an innovative software house, NeXtrope, which manages public and international projects that use the revolutionary Blockchain technology and an invitation to the BlockChain NeXt Conference which is the biggest event of this kind in CEE region. Unfortunately, this year's project is only for the citizens of Poland.
